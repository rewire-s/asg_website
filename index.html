About Midgard Online

Midgard Online is a Fantasy MMORPG in development by Asgard GameFi in which players control their NFT Avatars and adventure through the Nine Realms.

Our game combines the old-school feel of classic games with Web 3, ushering in a new experience never seen before.

Classes

There are many roles in Midgard, each with stats and abilities specific to their class. Choose the one that identifies you the most; warriors, sorcerers, artisans and merchants, among others. Modify your stats with experience points, collect equipment of your kind, and perform jobs of your affinity.

NFT Integration

Playable avatars and craftable items are unique to the Midgard universe, and represent active value on the Blockchain. Craft special items to sell, win quests to obtain them, or fight for loot in raids and PvP combat!

3D Art Inspired by  a 2D Retro Style

Aesthetics that fuse 3D environments with pixel-art elements inspired by classic video games (Chrono Trigger, Final Fantasy, Shining Soul, etc.)

Reputation System

In Midgard, your actions can be favorable or detrimental to the ecosystem, thus earning Odin's favor (FVOR) or arousing his wrath (WRTH). This directly impacts your economic functions, your character's attributes, and your social status within the community.

Participatory Economy

The internal economy of Midgard is constantly driven by the player, through the 3 tokens of the ecosystem (VOLT, MEAD and DRAC). The values and their availability change with each player's decision, favoring speculation and strategy.

Professions

The Midgard environment has many resources needed to craft weapons and equipment, and the player can enroll in a profession that favors obtaining and trading. Be a hunter, miner, fisherman, gatherer, farmer or wizard. Become a pro and earn abundant profits!
